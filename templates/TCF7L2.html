TCF7L2 Transcription Factor 7 like 2

Symbol: TCF7L2

Full Name: Transcription Factor 7 like 2

Organism: Homo sapiens

Ontology: The gene encodes a transcription factor involved in the Wnt signaling pathway. The protein functions as a transcriptional regulator of the expression of genes involved in lipid and glucose metabolism. Blood glucose homeostasis has been linked to the protein, therefore, genetic variations are associated to a greater risk of developing Type 2 Diabetes (T2D).
